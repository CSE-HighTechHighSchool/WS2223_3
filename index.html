What is VEX?

VEX is a robotics platform to engage high school students in robotics education. Students can use VEX parts to easily assemble robots to perform a wide variety of tasks. In addition to using VEX components for its curriculum, High Technology High School has three competitive VEX Robotics Competition (VRC) teams that construct robots to play a game and compete against other teams from across New Jersey and the world. This year, team 765A's, 765X's, and 765N's robots will be able to play a miniature version of disc golf on a 12' x 12' field.
